# Write the Heat: A Spice Scene Development Workbook

A hands-on workbook for romance and romantasy writers who want to plan, layer, and refine intimate scenes that serve their characters, advance their plots, and leave readers breathless.

## What This Is

This is a **writing workbook**, not a craft book. It is designed to be opened beside your manuscript and filled in as you plan, draft, and revise intimate scenes. Every section includes worksheets, checklists, and write-spaces for active use.

## Sections

| # | Section | What It Covers |
|---|---------|----------------|
| 1 | **Your Heat Level Compass** | Define your book's heat level (0-5) and commit to consistency |
| 2 | **Character Intimacy Profiles** | Map each character's desires, boundaries, vulnerability, and chemistry |
| 3 | **The 12 Stages of Physical Escalation** | Plan your characters' physical progression across the book |
| 4 | **The Five Layers of a Spice Scene** | Weave physical, emotional, sensory, thought, and dialogue layers |
| 5 | **Scene Pacing & Structure** | The three-act spice scene: anticipation, escalation, peak & release |

| 8 | **The Physics & Consent Audit** | Logistics checklist, fantastical elements audit, consent as craft |
| 9 | **Aftermath & Consequences** | Pillow talk, ripple effects, and the consequence tracker |
| 10 | **Scene Planning Worksheets** | Six blank worksheets pulling together every framework |
| App | **Appendices** | Sensory word banks, revision checklist, heat level quick reference |

## Available Formats

### Printable PDF (Current)
- **File:** `printable/spice-scene-workbook.html`
- **Tech:** HTML + CSS + Paged.js for print-ready PDF generation
- **Page size:** US Letter (8.5" x 11")
- **Estimated pages:** ~30 printed pages

## Generating the PDF

### Method 1: Browser Print
1. Open `printable/spice-scene-workbook.html` in Chrome
2. Wait for Paged.js to finish rendering (pages will appear with borders)
3. Press `Ctrl+P` / `Cmd+P`
4. Set destination to "Save as PDF"
5. Ensure "Background graphics" is checked
6. Save

### Method 2: Paged.js CLI
```bash
npx pagedjs-cli printable/spice-scene-workbook.html -o spice-scene-workbook.pdf
```
