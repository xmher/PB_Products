# The Shadow's Edge: A Morally Grey Love Interest Workbook

A specialized craft workbook for romantasy and dark romance authors who want to create psychologically complex, morally ambiguous love interests that readers can't stop thinking about.

## Product Overview

**Title:** The Shadow's Edge: A Morally Grey Love Interest Workbook
**Format:** Printable PDF (HTML + Paged.js)
**Target Page Count:** ~25 pages
**Target Price:** $12.00 - $15.00
**Target Audience:** Romantasy and dark romance authors

## Market Position

This workbook fills a gap in the current market where:
- Generic "villain" prompts exist but lack psychological depth
- Character planners are too broad for this specific archetype
- No dedicated tools address the "retcon prevention" problem
- Community language (Shadow Daddy, The Exception, etc.) is underserved

## Key Features

### Structural Tools (Not Just Prompts)
- **Moral Compass Scale** - Define the character's position on the grey spectrum (1-5)
- **Drive Rating System** - Rate psychological drives (Obsession, Power, Protection, Vengeance, Redemption)
- **Wound-to-Behavior Chain** - Reverse-engineer dark traits from formative trauma
- **Corrupted Syllogism Framework** - Build logical justification for "wrong" choices

### Consistency Mechanisms
- **Retcon Prevention Checklist** - Maintain darkness throughout romance arc
- **Edge Maintenance Log** - Track dark moments across the story
- **Non-Linear Redemption Tracking** - Plan realistic, messy character growth

### Reader Psychology Integration
- **The Exception Fantasy** - Map the internal/external behavioral split
- **Accountability Tracking** - Ensure consequences (social, emotional, physical)
- **Community Language Guide** - Use BookTok/market terminology effectively

## Sections

1. **Introduction: Beyond Hero or Villain** - The psychology of why readers love morally grey characters

4. **The Logic of Corruption** - Building internally consistent dark choices
5. **The Exception Fantasy** - The dangerous-to-world/soft-for-her dynamic
6. **Accountability & Consequences** - Making them pay for their choices
7. **Retcon Prevention** - Maintaining edge throughout the romance
8. **Character Blueprint Worksheets** - Comprehensive profile templates
9. **Archetype Gallery & Language Guide** - Examples and marketing terminology

### Generating PDF
Open `morally-grey-workbook.html` in a browser. Paged.js will automatically paginate the content for print. Use browser print (Cmd/Ctrl + P) and save as PDF.

**Recommended print settings:**
- Paper size: US Letter (8.5" x 11")
- Margins: Default (handled by CSS)
- Background graphics: ON (required for colors)
